Symptom

Some symptoms of COVID-19 include:

  • Cough
  • Fevers or chills
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Muscle pain
  • Sore throat
  • loss of taste and smell
  • Diarrhea
  • Headache
  • New fatigue
  • Nausea or vomitting
  • Congestion or runny noise

What to do if you have coronavirus

If you suspect you have coronavirus, please go get tested quickly.

If you do have coronavirus, please incubiate yourself for a minimum of 14 days. You should also wear a mask at all time when in public but also keep a 6ft distance from anybody else to avoid spreading.

You also need to practice safe hygience regularly like washing your names with hot water for 30 seconds!
